About the Tournament

Welcome to the KMNx League! The ultimate weekly tournament where the fiercest teams compete for glory. Every team consists of 5 skilled players battling across thrilling maps. Each week brings live matches, up-to-date stats, and action-packed gameplay. Teams earn 3 points per win and all kills are tracked to determine the ultimate champion.
