About the Tournament

Welcome to the KMNx League!
The ultimate weekly tournament where the fiercest teams compete for glory. Every team consists of 5 skilled players, battling it out across thrilling maps in intense matches.

Whether you’re here to follow your favorite team, track the live results, or see who dominates the leaderboard, the KMNx League has it all — live matches, up-to-date stats, and a schedule you can rely on.

Every victory counts: teams earn 3 points per win, while losses and kills are tracked to determine the ultimate champion at the end of the season.

Dive in, explore the league, and stay tuned — every week brings new action, new champions, and unforgettable moments.
